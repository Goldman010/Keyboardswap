# Decisions

## 2026-07-08

### Admin Approval

Decision:
Listings require manual admin approval before becoming public.

Reason:
Maintains marketplace quality, prevents spam, and allows new brands/products to be curated.

---

### Auctions

Decision:
Auction scheduling is handled by admins instead of sellers.

Reason:
Allows featured listings and coordinated auction launches.

---

### Bid Increments

Decision:
Fixed increments of $1, $2, $5, $10, and $25.

Reason:
Predictable experience while allowing flexibility for different item values.

---

### Brands

Decision:
Users may suggest new brands.

Reason:
Keeps the catalog growing while preserving data quality through admin review.
